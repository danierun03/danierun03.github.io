About danierun

Daniel Joy Abarca Baguio, also known online as danierun or jan Tanijelun, is a simple guy who likes to create stuff and be fun. He has drawn stuff from a young age, has been an avid Hatsune Miku fan since 2017, and has been gradually establishing his online presence ever since. He's currently a student of Bachelor of Science in Information Technology at University of Mindanao - Tagum College, and dreams to be a recognizeable illustrator and musician someday.

Sciences (Chemistry, Physics, Astronomy, Anatomy, Linguistics, etc.), History, some Politics, Arts, Music, Anime (especially Vocaloid), Memes, Computers, etc.
Languages I'm currently fairly fluent at
English, Tagalog, Cebuano, Japanese, Toki Pona (a minimalist constructed language)
Hobbies
Drawing, Listening to music, Watching interesting videos, Playing, Going out to somewhere, Cooking, Trying what I've been wanting to try
