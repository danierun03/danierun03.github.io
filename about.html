About danierun

danierun (any pronouns) (also known as danierun03, danierundesu, or jan Tanijelun) is a simple genderfluid femboy who likes to create stuff and have fun. He has drawn stuff from a young age, has been an avid Hatsune Miku fan since 2017, and has been gradually establishing his online presence ever since. He's currently a BSIT student and dreams to be a recognizeable illustrator and musician someday.
