Rooms and Other Facilities

Besides gathering at the cottages, guests have the option to stay at overnight rooms or gather with more people at the function hall.

Overnight Rooms

Rooms for guests to lodge overnight
  • ₱1,500 per night
  • Good for 4 people
These rooms offer a choice to guests who decide to enjoy the facilities overnight. Built atop of so-called "M/V Coolen's", the rooms feature beds, a personal desk, and its own personal bathroom.
  • ₱1,500 per night
  • Good for 4 people

Function Hall

Function hall for hosting large events
  • ₱3,000 per day
  • Holds approx. 100 people
The function hall is great for groups who want to host a large event inside the resort. The hall is complete with a stage, a podium, and long table sets for catering programmes and activities.
  • ₱3,000 per day
  • Holds approx. 100 people
