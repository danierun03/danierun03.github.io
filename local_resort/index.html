Coolen's Garden resort is a place to enjoy occassions, holidays, celebrations, and lots of free time! With various pools, cottages, overnight rooms, mini-shop, and other amenities, this place will give you a splashy experience at an affordable price!

We showcase Adult Pools and Kiddie Pools all around the establishment for a enjoyable, splashy swimming experience. We also have showers for people to bathe in before and after having fun within the pools.

We also have our own Shop for our guests to buy beverages, snacks, and amenities for a satisfying resort experience. No need to getting far away outside just to buy what you need for the outing.

Last but not the least, we offer other activities such as boating and looking around the scenery around the resort.
