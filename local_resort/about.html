About Us

Coolen's Garden Resort was established in 2012, and has since expanded its facilities and services for recreation and bonding. We are dedicated to be a resort that's safe, family-friendly, and fun, all-year long!

Coolen's Garden Resort is located in Feeder Road 3, Purok 14, Barangay Tibal-og, Santo Tomas, Davao del Norte.
